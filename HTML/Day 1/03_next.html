Changes in HTML 5

<b> and <i> have been deprecated in HTML5 although they still work in Chrome. There are now other ways to accomplish this with Cascading Style Sheets. <strong> and <em> are semantic markups and affect things like speech readers, whereas <b> and <i> do not. Most browsers chose to display <strong> as bold and <em> as italicized, but this is a choice.
